Crypt Gallery

An unusual gallery. The crypt beneath St Pancras Church homes many unique pieces of artwork. The site initailly was used to bury Londoners in the 19th century, but since 2002 it has hosted contemporary art.

DKUK Salon

Artist and hairdresser Daniel Kelly opened this very unique gallery-salon hybrid. Enjoy the artwork as you get your hair turned into a work of art itself!

Cabinet

Featured are huge canvase painted by artists that are part-theatrical backdrop, part-public display of battles or romantic scenes and more. A very secretive place and you must make an appointment online.
