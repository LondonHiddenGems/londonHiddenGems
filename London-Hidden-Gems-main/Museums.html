THE POSTAL MUSEUM

It was opened in 2004 as the British Postal Museum & archive by the Postal Heritage Trust. It will take you through the history of postal services, revealing really interesting fun facts about our mail. If you visit it, you will also have the chance to ride through the hidden tunnels or walk through the 100-year-old mail rail tunnels. So, do not miss this one.

THE FREUD MUSEUM

It is a museum dedicated to the famous neurologist and it is in the same place where he lived with his family. So it is definitely worth taking a look.

THE CARTOON MUSEUM

This is definitely a very fun museum, where you can learn a bit more of the history of cartoons, caricatures, comic strips, and animation. It has lots of colours and it is owned by the Cartoon Art Trust

THE MUSEUM OF BRANDS

Come and join the nostalgic journey of 200 years of change. You will learn and be able to see how the culture and lifestyle has changed shown by the re-branding over the years. It even goes to the Victorian times.
